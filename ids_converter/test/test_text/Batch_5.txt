【Specification 23】Footing
IFC Version: IFC4X3_ADD2
Description: Footings are typically described geometrically by enclosed polygonal areas extruded
vertically according to footing depth. For stepped footings, multiple extruded solids may be used, however they must not intersect.

Missing: Geometry, Connection to Piles, Reinforcing

Piles supporting footings are linked according to a connection relationship.
Reinforcing is typically modelled as aggregation of Footing - Footing/Reinforcement

Applicability:
  Cardinality: 0..unbounded
  • Applies to all IFCFOOTING type entities

Requirements:
  1. Part Of Requirement
     - Relation Type: IFCRELCONTAINEDINSPATIALSTRUCTURE
     - Related Entity: IFCBRIDGEPART
     - Predefined Type: Must be one of: SUBSTRUCTURE, FOUNDATION
     - Cardinality: Required
  2. Attribute Requirement
     - Attribute Name: Name
     - Cardinality: Required
     - Instructions: Name of the footing for referencing purposes, as would be found on construction plans.
  3. Material Requirement
     - Cardinality: Required
     - Instructions: Material properties of the footing, indicating concrete strength.


【Specification 24】Pile
IFC Version: IFC4X3_ADD2
Description: Piles are typically described geometrically by a circular profile extruded vertically according to pile depth. For multiple piles, mapped representation may be used to efficiently place piles of similar dimensions in multiple locations.

Missing: Cross section, Batter

Piles supporting footings are linked according to a connection relationship.

Applicability:
  Cardinality: 0..unbounded
  • Applies to all IFCPILE type entities

Requirements:
  1. Part Of Requirement
     - Relation Type: IFCRELCONTAINEDINSPATIALSTRUCTURE
     - Related Entity: IFCBRIDGEPART
     - Predefined Type: SUBSTRUCTURE
     - Cardinality: Required
  2. Attribute Requirement
     - Attribute Name: Name
     - Cardinality: Required
     - Instructions: Name of pile arrangement for referencing purposes, as found on construction plans.
  3. Material Requirement
     - Cardinality: Required
     - Instructions: Material of piles.
