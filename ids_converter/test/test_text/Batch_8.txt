【Specification 36】Parapet
IFC Version: IFC4X3_ADD2
Description: This entity may be used to model barriers of constant cross-section, or architectural railings.

Barriers may be defined as a constant cross-section placed along the alignment at either edge of a bridge deck or anywhere in between.

Comment: Geometry, Connections, Reinforcing, Conduit

Applicability:
  Cardinality: 0..unbounded
  • Applies to all IFCWALL type entities

Requirements:
  1. Part Of Requirement
     - Relation Type: IFCRELCONTAINEDINSPATIALSTRUCTURE
     - Related Entity: IFCBRIDGEPART
     - Predefined Type: Must be one of: DECK, DECK_SEGMENT
     - Cardinality: Required
  2. Attribute Requirement
     - Attribute Name: Name
     - Cardinality: Required
     - Instructions: Name of the parapet segment for referencing purposes as would be found on construction plans.
  3. Material Requirement
     - Cardinality: Required
     - Instructions: Material of the parapet.
